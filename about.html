Our Story

WHY WE
EXIST

"Miami's exotic car scene deserves a detailer that actually matches its standards."

The dream was always racing. It still is.

But I learned early that the fastest way into this world isn't always through the track — it's through the cars and the people behind them. Every exotic vehicle has an owner with a story, a network, and a passion that runs just as deep as mine.

BH Car Detailing is my entry point. A way to get close to the machines I've always loved, understand them inside and out, and build real relationships within a world I intend to be a part of for life.

We treat every car we touch with that same obsession — because to us, this isn't a job. It's the beginning of something much bigger.
