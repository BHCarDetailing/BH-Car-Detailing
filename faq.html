Service & Booking
The easiest way is through our online booking wizard — it takes under 3 minutes. Select your service, tell us about your vehicle, enter your contact info and address, then pick a time via Calendly. You can also call or text us at (917) 783-1038.
We recommend booking 24–48 hours in advance to secure your preferred time slot. Same-day bookings are sometimes available — call or text us to check. For ceramic coating or correction services, give us at least 3–5 days notice.
If it's your first detail with us, the Full Detail is the most popular starting point. If your car has swirls, scratches, or dull paint, look into Correction + Coating. Not sure? Text us a photo of your car and we'll recommend the right service for its condition.
Yes — use the link in your Calendly confirmation email to reschedule or cancel. We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. For same-day cancellations, please call or text us directly.
Mobile Service
We serve all of Miami-Dade County and into South Broward — including Brickell, Miami Beach, Coral Gables, Coconut Grove, Aventura, Sunny Isles, Surfside, Bal Harbour, North Miami, Hollywood, and Fort Lauderdale. Not sure if we cover your area? Just text us your zip code.
All we need is access to water (a standard outdoor hose connection) and a 110v power outlet within reasonable distance of your vehicle. We bring every tool, product, bucket, vacuum, and supply needed. You don't have to do anything.
Yes — we detail in driveways, garages, surface lots, and covered parking. For covered garages, we'll confirm water and power access during booking. Interior-only services can be done without any water source.
We only ask that you're available to hand over access at the start and inspect the results at the end — that's it. You don't need to watch us work. Many clients go about their day while we take care of everything.
Pricing & Payment
Our prices are listed starting from a base rate. Final pricing can vary based on vehicle size (SUV vs. coupe), current condition (heavily soiled vehicles may require additional time), and any add-ons. We'll always confirm the final price before starting work.
Payment is collected only after the job is complete and you have personally inspected and approved the results. We don't take deposits on standard services — you pay when you're satisfied.
We accept Zelle, Venmo, Cash, Apple Pay, and major credit/debit cards. Payment details are confirmed during booking or at the time of service.
Services & Results
Timing depends on the service and your vehicle's current condition:

BH Detail: 3–5 hrs

We'll give you a precise estimate when you book.
Paint correction is a machine-polishing process that removes swirl marks, light scratches, water spots, and oxidation from your paint's clear coat — restoring it to a mirror-like finish. If you see swirls in sunlight or your paint looks dull, you're a candidate. We'll assess your paint during a consultation.
For daily drivers: a full detail every 6–8 weeks and a maintenance refresh every 3–4 weeks is ideal. For exotics driven less frequently: a full detail every 3 months and a monthly check-in keeps the paint and interior in top condition. Ask us about our maintenance plans for recurring clients.
Yes. BH Car Detailing carries full liability insurance. Your vehicle is fully covered from the moment we arrive until we pack up and leave. We've detailed Ferraris, Lamborghinis, McLarens, Rolls-Royces, and more — without incident.
We use professional-grade, pH-neutral, paint-safe products throughout — brands trusted by detailers worldwide for high-end vehicles. No harsh chemicals, no shortcuts. If you have specific product preferences or restrictions for your vehicle, just let us know when you book.
